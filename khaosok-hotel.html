The Cliff River & Jungle Resort Khao Sok
The Cliff & River Jungle Resort is is located in the spectacularly beautiful Khao Sok National Park, which covers more than 700 km2. The park is home to many rare species of birds and wildlife and is the largest of its kind in Thailand.
 Khao Sok Rain Forest Resort
 Khaosok Rainforest Resort is beside the river and you can go to swim to be cool and enjoy the tropical nature around you. Our bungalows have some great views of the jungle covered mountains around us that are alive with color and sounds.
We offer the bungalows with electric fan and air-conditioning. All of the air-conditioning rooms are fully furnished which contain TV, mini bar, English toilet, hot shower, mosquito net, balconies and a lovely natural decorating style in bathroom.
Khao Sok Paradise Resort
Khao Sok eco Resort situates in a privately owned area, quiet and peaceful. You will be welcome by our friendly staff that is hired locally in order to help the community. We offer distinctive leisure activities in Khao Sok such as the Chiew larn lake trip, jungle treks, elephant treks, canoeing, jungle survival trips and much more. Let us be part of your unforgettable experience in Thailand and make the journey of your life time here in Khao Sok.

 

 Our Jungle House Nature Resort
Our Khao Sok treehouses, bungalows and comfortable cottages offer a nice selection of scenery and experiences. Your treehouse or bungalow accommodation will look out over a clear river stream to majestic limestone cliffs, surrounded by natural jungle.
